About me

The who, the what

My name is Stian Malmin, design enthusiast and future front-end developer and product developer Design is something i am passionate enough about to devote my life to. The pleasuring activity of forming ideas and dive into them, experimenting with and optimizing tangible objects with my tools. Thats how it started anyhow, that was before i was consciously thinking about the user journey that every user goes through utilizing a product, from their perspective. And they are many. After having gone through product design studies, and web design studies, the meaning of the word design has been dramatically broadened, and i have learned not how to make things look pretty, a skill i already possessed, but what design really is, ways of analyzing problems and tools that makes the process of developing so much more thorough than what i had before..
